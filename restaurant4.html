Our Story

Every Flavor Tells a Story

We take pride in serving the finest Asian cuisine, featuring homemade and authentic dishes. One of our specialties is our breakfast, which includes dishes like hummus loaded with almonds, pomegranate seeds, and pita bread. But our menu does not stop at breakfast. We also offer a wide range of kebab plates, including chicken, all grilled to perfection over charcoal. Our dips and sauces,are the perfect complement to any meal; and for dessert, we offer a variety of homemade pastries and sweets. At Foodday, we strive to create a welcoming atmosphere for our customers to enjoy delicious food and experience the rich culture of the place. Whether you are joining us for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, we look forward to sharing our passion for food with you.
